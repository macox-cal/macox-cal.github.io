New Front Lawn in Carbis Bay

This project was on a steeply sloping site in Carbis Bay. To level the front lawn, we built a retaining wall on 3 sides, in-filled with rubble and then topsoil, and finally laid new turf. We also laid paving slabs on steps up to a patio, and on the patio itself.
